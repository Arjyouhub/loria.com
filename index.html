About Loria Trading

Loria Trading was established in Dubai in January 1996 with the aim of providing clients in the hospitality industry a creative design and fabrication service resulting in distinctively unique products. Loria offers a wide range of items, including; Trays, Bowls, Stands and Servers in a variety of materials and designs. The Loria Heritage line, includes a selection of culturally inspired items such as; Arabic Coffee Dallahs, Incense burners, Moroccan Tea & Turkish Coffee service. These are displayed and sold in a dedicated showroom in the Khan Murian area of Dubai's Wafi City
