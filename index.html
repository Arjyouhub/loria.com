About Loria Trading

Loria Trading specializes in the design, fabrication, and marketing of premium hospitality products. All items are fabricated in-house using plated brass, stainless steel, acrylic, and wood.

Introduction

The product range includes hollowware, flatware, banqueting tables, and food display solutions. Loria Trading presents the Arab culture inspired Heritage Collection along with a popular assortment of Moroccan items. The Loria Heritage showroom is located at Wafi City – Khan Mourjan, Dubai.

Mission

To meet the specific requirements of hospitality clients by providing products that can be modified, customized, or created as new designs to perfectly fit their needs.

Vision

To become a leading provider of custom and standardized hospitality products across the Middle East, including Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Oman, and Lebanon.
